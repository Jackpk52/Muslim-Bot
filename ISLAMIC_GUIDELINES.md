# Islamic Guidelines for Muslim Bot

## Purpose
This document outlines the Islamic principles and guidelines that govern the development and use of Muslim Bot.

## Core Islamic Values

### 1. Authenticity
- All Islamic content must be verified from authentic sources
- Cite sources for Quran verses and Hadith
- Avoid personal interpretations without scholarly backing

### 2. Respect
- Treat all Islamic symbols and practices with respect
- Avoid controversial theological debates
- Focus on common Islamic principles

### 3. Accuracy
- Ensure prayer time calculations are accurate
- Provide correct Islamic information
- Regular updates for changing calculations

### 4. Inclusivity
- Respect all Islamic schools of thought
- Avoid sectarian language
- Focus on unity of the Ummah

## Content Guidelines

### Permitted Content:
- Quran verses with proper translation attribution
- Authentic Hadith with proper referencing
- Basic Islamic teachings from reliable sources
- Prayer times and Islamic calendar information

### Prohibited Content:
- Personal fatwas or religious rulings
- Controversial theological debates
- Disrespect towards any Islamic school
- Unverified Islamic information

## Source Requirements

### Primary Sources:
- Quran (with recognized translations)
- Sahih Bukhari and Muslim
- Other authentic Hadith collections
- Recognized Islamic scholars' works

### Verification Process:
1. Check source authenticity
2. Verify translation accuracy
3. Ensure proper context
4. Review by knowledgeable individuals

## Implementation Notes

### Prayer Times:
- Use established calculation methods
- Provide method information to users
- Allow for regional differences

### Islamic Content:
- Keep explanations simple and clear
- Provide references for all information
- Avoid complex theological discussions

## Continuous Improvement

We commit to:
- Regular review of Islamic content
- Updating with new authentic information
- Responding to community feedback
- Maintaining high standards of accuracy
